# Privacy Policy - Subxtract

_Last Updated: March 16, 2025_

## Introduction

Welcome to Subscription Tracker ("we," "our," or "us"). We understand that privacy is important to you, and we are committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how your information is collected, used, and disclosed when you use our Subscription Tracker application ("App").

## Summary

- Our App operates primarily **offline** and stores all of your subscription data **locally on your device**
- We do not collect, store, or transmit your subscription information to any external servers
- No advertising or third-party analytics are implemented
- You maintain complete control of your data

## Information Collection and Use

### Information You Provide

Our App collects the following information that you manually enter:

- Subscription service names
- Payment amounts
- Billing cycles
- Renewal dates
- Payment methods
- Related subscription notes

### Local Storage

**All data entered into the App is stored locally on your device.** We do not collect, transmit, or store any of your subscription information on external servers or in the cloud. Your information remains exclusively on your device unless you explicitly choose to export or back up your data using the device's built-in sharing mechanisms.

### Analytics and Crash Reporting

The App does not implement any third-party analytics services. We do not track your usage patterns, activity within the App, or any personal information.

## Data Retention and Deletion

Since all data is stored locally on your device, you maintain complete control over your information. You can delete any or all of your subscription data at any time through the App's interface. When you uninstall the App, all associated data is removed from your device (unless you've created external backups).
